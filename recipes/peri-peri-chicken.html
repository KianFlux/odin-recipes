Peri Peri Chicken and Corn Cobs

Description

Peri Peri chicken is a flavorful and spicy dish that is sure to tantalize your taste buds. Marinated in a blend of spicy peri peri sauce, garlic, and herbs, the chicken is grilled to perfection, resulting in a juicy and tender texture.

The fiery flavors of peri peri sauce are balanced by the aromatic garlic and herbs, creating a mouth-watering taste sensation. This dish can be served with a variety of sides, such as salad, rice, or roasted vegetables, making it a versatile and satisfying meal for any occasion.

If you're looking to add some excitement to your dinner table, Peri Peri chicken is sure to do the trick. It's a delicious and easy-to-prepare dish that will leave you and your guests craving more!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons chilli flakes (or to taste)
  • 4 cloves garlic crushed or grated
  • Juice of 2 limes
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoons oregano
  • Â½ teaspoon salt (or to taste)
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 8 chicken legs (or thighs or a mixture)
  • 4 small corn on the cobs (cobettes)
  • 1 serving of rice (suggestion)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 220C / 200C / gas mark 7 / 425F. (Save this step till later if you plan to marinate your chicken first)
  2. Mix together all of the Peri Peri Chicken ingredients (apart from the corn on the cobs and the actual chicken!) in a large bowl. Score the skin of the chicken with the point of a sharp knife and place the chicken in the bowl of marinade, turning to coat each piece of chicken with marinade.
  3. Marinate the chicken for 1 hour in the fridge or up to 24 hours if you have the time. Alternatively just continue with the recipe.
  4. Place all the chicken pieces on a roasting tray and place the tray in your preheated oven.
  5. Roast the chicken for 30 minutes, remove the tray from the oven, add the corn on the cobs turning them in the juices, then return the tray to the oven for a further 10 minutes.
  6. Arrange your selection of rice on a serving platter, top with the chicken pieces and corn on the cobs. Drizzle over any juices from the chicken pan and then enjoy your yummy chicken meal!
